Full version

        "Hamro Opportunity" was a year-long project that started with the vision of two individuals and grew into a team of 28 dedicated members. Over the course of one year, our primary objective was to provide Nepali high school seniors with effortless access to information regarding a diverse array of extracurricular events, competitions, and workshops.



Challenges Addressed:

Throughout this year, the project confronted a central issue: students had to search extensively online for event information, often with little success. Our solution involved not only gathering event details but also ensuring that our platform's pages appeared prominently in search engine results, eliminating the need for students to search far and wide.



Solution Provided:

During this year, we diligently collected information from various sources and integrated it into a single platform, effectively streamlining access to event details. Additionally, our project involved collaborating with students from different colleges to broaden our reach, making event information accessible even in rural areas of Nepal.



Target Audience:

High school seniors were our primary focus over the year, as they stood to gain the most from the platform's comprehensive information, contributing to their personal and academic growth.



Project Uniqueness:

In the span of this year, our project's unique approach lay in uniting various platforms to reach the right audience, much like Amazon connects customers and sellers, except without any associated fees.



Impact:

Throughout the year, our project succeeded in enabling students to excel in their respective fields by facilitating their participation in various events. This contributed not only to their academic growth but also nurtured their passions and personal development through easy access to event information.



Achievements:

During this year, we are proud to have reached over 2000 students from diverse regions of Nepal, extending beyond the Terai region. Our project made a substantial impact on student opportunities and growth, ensuring they were well-informed about event opportunities.




Official Launch in July 2022:

On 16, we officially launched "Hamro Opportunity." This launch was marked by a simple social media post, which was the first step in introducing our vision to the world.



Designing the Website:

Before the official launch of this project, our founder was actively involved in the website design process. He dedicated nearly three months to designing the website using the WordPress platform. Creating a website from scratch was a significant undertaking, and it could have taken years to complete. Instead, our founder chose to work on customizing a template to save time and effort. Before the launch, we sought feedback and revisions from experts in the field. The result was a website that catered to the needs of high school students, providing easy navigation, and categorizing events and opportunities for classes 9, 10, 11, and 12. To ensure the smooth operation of the project, we opened applications for team executives and members. [ Dipesh, Diwas, Kishor, and Navodit] interviewed more than 50 interested participants. Expanding the Platform:

After a successful year of "Hamro Opportunity," we're thrilled to announce that we're in the final stages of the design phase for our enhanced platform. This comprehensive resource hub is taking shape, and we're actively working on adding content about national and international scholarships and academic resources.

For a sneak peek at the current design, you can visit the link below. Stay tuned for further updates as we continue to empower students on their academic journey.



Time Management:

Our team faced time management challenges due to the demanding academic commitments of our high school executive members. This resulted in occasional delays in posting information on our website, especially during exams and holidays. To address this, we implemented strategies such as prioritization, scheduling, delegation, and flexibility. We emphasized the importance of balancing academics and project work, created schedules that accounted for exam periods and holidays, delegated tasks based on availability and expertise, and maintained flexibility in project deadlines. This allowed us to effectively manage our time without compromising the project's quality.



Technology Familiarity:

In the initial stages, team members faced difficulties in understanding the technology and methodology implemented on the website. To bridge this knowledge gap, we organized workshops to provide hands-on learning experiences. Additionally, we created comprehensive guide videos to offer clear instructions on the technology and methodology used. These resources allowed team members to gain a better understanding of the project's technological aspects.





Uniform Graphics Design :

Achieving a consistent and visually appealing template for social media posts presented a challenge. To ensure uniform graphics design, we collaborated closely with our graphics designers. Their expertise in creating visually engaging graphics played a vital role in maintaining consistency and quality.





Effective Communication :

Effective communication with team members was crucial for project success. There were instances where we needed to connect with team members to address issues and ensure smooth project operations. We maintained open lines of communication within the team. Regular team meetings, updates, and an accessible communication platform allowed us to address issues promptly. Team members could raise concerns, seek assistance, and collaborate effectively, ensuring that the project ran smoothly.



        Our success was a testament to the hard work and commitment of our exceptional team members. Each member brought a unique set of skills and experiences to the project, contributing to our shared vision of providing extracurricular event opportunities to Nepali high school seniors.
